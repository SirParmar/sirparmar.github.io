ABOUT ME

Scion of the Ujjainiya House of Parmar Rajputs

Manish Singh Parmar is born to Shriji Rajendra Prasad Singh and Smt. Rupali Devi Singh of Deoghar on October 25th, 2001 (Ashwina Shukla MahaNavami 2058).
